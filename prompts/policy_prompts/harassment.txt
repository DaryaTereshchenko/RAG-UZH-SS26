# Criteria

## Overview

This policy classifies content that constitutes harassment or bullying directed at individuals.

## Definition of Terms

- Harassment: Conduct intended to intimidate, degrade, or threaten an individual through verbal, written, or physical means.
- Bullying: Repeated or severe aggressive behavior targeting a specific person to demean, humiliate, or intimidate them.
- Doxing: Revealing or threatening to reveal private or sensitive personal information without consent.
- Coordinated Harassment: Organized efforts by multiple individuals to target and harass a person.

## Interpretation of Language

- Profanity or obscene language alone does not constitute harassment; it must be directed at degrading, insulting, or threatening a specific person.
- Consider the target: language directed at a specific individual is more likely to qualify than general expressive profanity.
- Threats that are conditional or implied still qualify if they communicate intent to harm or intimidate.

## Definition of Labels

### (HB): Harassment and Bullying

#### Includes

- Insulting someone or threatening to insult someone, including using profanity or obscene language to degrade them
- Showing, promoting, or threatening harassment or bullying (physical or otherwise) of others, including coordinated harassment
- Doxing, blackmailing, revealing, or calling for the reveal of private or sensitive information

#### Excludes

- Unedited movie or TV clips
- Content using offensive language in clearly satirical or comedic contexts without genuine harmful intent
- Content criticizing people in positions of significant public power (politicians, police, public officials) focusing on their actions or policies rather than targeting protected attributes
- Content using potentially offensive language where the speaker is a member of the same group being referenced
- Educational or documentary content with appropriate framing that does not promote harmful behavior
- Content that may be problematic but does not specifically constitute harassment or bullying as defined above
